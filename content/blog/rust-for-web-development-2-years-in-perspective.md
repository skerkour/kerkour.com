## Conclusion

Rust is the last programming language I learned. Ever.
It is so versatile that even if web development was not its initially targeted niche, it's a real pleasure to work with, and I strongly recommend it. It's a long journey to become effective, but totally worth it: your web services will become so reliable that you'll finally be able to sleep well and your response times so small that your users will fall in love with your product.
