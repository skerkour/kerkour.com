## Benefits

The is many benefits to have all my stuff fitting in only to bags. Here is a few which come to my mind right now:

* Don't have to pay for checked luggage
* Ability to relocate in less than 20 minutes
* All my stuff is high quality. If something is damaged, it's replaced

## Drawbacks
